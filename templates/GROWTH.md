## How to Use

1. Run `/apple:growth` to create and populate this scorecard (`--new` pre-launch seeds it as a launch plan).
2. Re-run quarterly or after each major release — deltas land in **Audit History**; only Status / Evidence / Next-step cells change.
3. Work items through the **Route** column commands; the **Top 5** table is the working queue.
4. Never reorder or renumber rows — item IDs are the diff key across audits.
5. The **Recurring Calendar** and **Top 5** are maintained by `/apple:growth`, not by hand; overdue calendar rows jump into the Top 5 automatically.
